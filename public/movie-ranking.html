The Best Movies of All Time

My top 3 movies of all time

A League of Their Own

Wonderful movie about the success of women in sports in the 1940's.

Avengers: End Game

Could anything be better than the death of a hero like Iron Man?

Star Trek: First Contact

It's nice to think about a time when we actually survive a nuclear war and live to see
contact with an alien species that doesn't want to use us for experimentation or harvest
our natural resources.
